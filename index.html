Short Bio

Buck Wise is an entrepreneur, speaker, and brand architect who has driven $842M+ in marketing results for Nike, Starbucks, Google, Grant Cardone, and Ryan Serhant. He is the Founder and CEO of Qinary, a full-service personal brand agency. Buck speaks on personal branding, revenue growth, and AI optimization.

Full Bio

Buck Wise has spent two decades at the intersection of branding, media, and revenue. He built and sold two agencies (acquired by WPP), served as SVP at Wunderman Thompson running Fortune 500 campaigns, held the CMO role at Grant Cardone Ventures where he built a marketing engine that drove $62M+ in revenue in under two years, and led brand and revenue strategy for Ryan Serhant's ventures, delivering 300% revenue growth. His resume includes social strategy for Nike Running, Nike Golf, and Nike Women, campaign strategy for Starbucks, innovation work with Google, and 82+ market syndicated radio alongside Ryan Seacrest on iHeartRadio. Today, as Founder and CEO of Qinary, Buck builds personal brands for founders, executives, and public figures across social, content, PR, and AI search optimization. He speaks on turning personal brand into revenue, the authority formula, data-driven growth, and becoming the expert that AI recommends.
